Here's a little information on my Sushi Recipe

1. To prepare the ingredients, it would take approximately 1hr and 30mins. Preparing the rice would take 1hr and 15mins.
2. Then cooking it would take 15 minutes.
3. Only serves 1 person
4. It's so easy for a beginner

Here are the ingredients for the Sushi Dish

For our Sushi dish, we will be using the below

  • Sushi rice (1/2 cup)
  • Nori sheets (1 sheet)
  • Salmon (2 sliced)
  • Avocado (1/2)
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Tools to be used too

  • - Bamboo sushi mat
  • - Sharp knife
  • - Cutting board

Extras

  • Soy Sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled Ginger

Here I would be covering step by step instructions on to make your Sushi

Steps:

  1. Prepare Sushi Rice:
    - Rinse the rice thoroughly and cook according to package instructions.
    - Season the cooked rice with a mixture of 1 tablespoon rice vinegar, 1 teaspoon sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon salt and let it cool to room temperature.

  2. Prepare Fillings:
    - Slice your avocado and salmon into thin strips.

  3. Assemble Sushi:
    - Place a nori sheet flat on the bamboo sushi mat.
    - Spread the sushi rice evenly over the nori, leaving about 1 inch of space at the top.
    - Place your fillings horizontally in the middle of the rice.

  4. Roll Sushi:
    - Using the mat, roll the sushi tightly but gently, applying even pressure.
    - Once rolled, apply a bit of water to the top edge of the nori to seal the roll.

  5. Slice Sushi:
    - Using a sharp knife, slice the roll into 6-8 pieces, depending on your preference.

  6. Serve:
    - Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger if desired.
